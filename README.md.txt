Overview 

The project explores employee-related data to uncover insights about workforce distribution, salary patterns, and correlations between various factors such as age, position, and team. The analysis focuses on understanding organizational structure, identifying key trends, and deriving data-driven insights that can support HR and management decisions.

Analyzed how employees are distributed across various job roles, identified departments with the highest number of specific positions.

Conclusion:
This analysis provides a clear overview of workforce structure and compensation trends within the organization. It helps management identify staffing imbalances, plan hiring strategies, and understand how experience influences pay.
